About Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Nana's Kimchi is a renowned destination for authentic Korean dining and traditional kimchi. Experience the true flavor of Korean culture through our meticulously crafted dishes. We bring high-quality, aged kimchi to your table, made with care and tradition.

History of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Founded by Nana Shim in the vibrant heart of the city, our restaurant has been a staple of Korean culinary excellence since 1980. Nana's passion for kimchi and Korean food shaped her dream into a celebrated culinary tradition.

The Staff of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to deliver delicious dishes with a smile. Trained in traditional Korean cooking methods, they embody the spirit of hospitality and authenticity.

Fresh Ingredients

We take pride in sourcing only the freshest ingredients, ensuring that every dish is vibrant and flavorful. From locally grown vegetables to aged kimchi and premium cuts of Samgyupsal, we guarantee an authentic taste of Korea.

The Warm Hospitality of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

At Nana's, we believe in creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. Our friendly staff and cozy atmosphere ensure your dining experience is unforgettable.

Mission of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Our mission is to share Korea's rich culinary heritage with the world. We aim to create connections through food, fostering an appreciation for traditional flavors and cooking methods.

The Ambiance

The restaurant boasts an eclectic Korean-themed design, offering both air-conditioned and al fresco dining areas. Its vibrant and cozy atmosphere makes it the perfect spot for casual meals and special occasions alike.

Location

Visit Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant in downtown for an unforgettable dining experience.

Operating Hours: 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM
