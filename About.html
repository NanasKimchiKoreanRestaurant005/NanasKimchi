About Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant in Roxas City offers an authentic Korean dining experience, featuring meticulously crafted dishes that showcase the rich flavors of Korean culture. Their menu includes high-quality, aged kimchi, prepared with care and tradition, providing a true taste of Korea.

The restaurant's ambiance complements its culinary offerings, with eclectic Korean-themed décor and both air-conditioned and al fresco dining areas. This vibrant and cozy setting makes it an ideal spot for casual meals and special occasions alike.

History of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Founded by Nana Shim in the vibrant heart of the city, Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant has been serving authentic Korean cuisine since 1980. Inspired by her love for traditional kimchi-making and her grandmother's recipes, Nana created a space where the rich flavors of Korea could be celebrated. Her passion for quality ingredients and time-honored techniques quickly earned the restaurant a reputation as a local favorite.

Over the decades, Nana's has grown from a humble family eatery into a cherished destination for lovers of Korean food. Whether enjoying our signature kimchi, sizzling samgyupsal, or a variety of banchan, every dish reflects our commitment to preserving the authentic tastes of Korea while creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all.

The Staff of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

At Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant, our team is the heart and soul of our establishment. Led by owner Reina Chrisatal "Nana" Shin, each member is meticulously trained in traditional Korean cooking methods, ensuring that every dish reflects the authentic flavors of Korea. Their unwavering commitment to excellence and passion for culinary arts are evident in the delightful meals they prepare and serve daily.

With warm smiles and attentive service, they strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where guests feel like part of our extended family. Fresh Ingredients

At Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant, we are committed to delivering an authentic Korean dining experience by using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients. We source locally grown vegetables, ensuring that each dish is vibrant and flavorful. Our kimchi is prepared in-house, following traditional fermentation methods to achieve its distinctive taste and health benefits.

We take pride in offering premium cuts of meat, such as our succulent samgyupsal (pork belly), which are carefully selected to provide the best texture and flavor. By combining these top-tier ingredients with time-honored Korean cooking techniques, we guarantee an authentic and satisfying culinary journey for our guests.

Mission of Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant

Our mission at Nana's Kimchi & Korean Restaurant is to share the rich and vibrant culinary heritage of Korea with our community and beyond. We are committed to preserving the authenticity of traditional Korean flavors while creating a welcoming space where people can connect over hearty meals and memorable experiences. Through every dish we serve, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for Korea's culture, promote the value of high-quality ingredients, and bring people together in celebration of good food and warm hospitality.
